Q&A

What skating skills should those wishing to learn have?
The programme is designed for skating children from 5 to 14 years old. The minimum level is a confident, controlled downhill on green slopes. If your child is a little younger, skates well and can handle the whole day, they can also be included in the camp, after a test run. If your child's skiing skills are a little lower than they should be, our instructors will be able to prepare them individually or in groups. for our camp.
How old can I be in the camp?
Depending on your child's physical condition and skills. After individual testing with our coaches, participation from 4-5 years old is possible. Our instructors can, in just a few one-to-one lessons, prepare your child quickly prepare your child for ski camp.
What if I don't have skiing equipment?
All necessary equipment at various levels, including a safety helmet, can be rented from our rentals. Special discounts are available for course participants.
